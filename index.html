Volunteers

Every year, the Big Event is blessed to have over 1500+ Quinnipiac Students Volunteer go out and help over 100+ non-profits in Connecticut. This is an amazing oppurtunity to go out and give back to the community that is focused on making those around them - happy.

Getting involved is easy and only takes up about three to four hours of your time. It may seem like nothing, but it means everything to those who need the extra hands to keep their non-profit running!
